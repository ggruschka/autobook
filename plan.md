## Planned Edits

### Edit 1: Give Isa her own scene (intercut during Guille's quest)
**Resolves**: structure[1], characters[1]
Add a scene where Isa wakes, finds Guille gone, and must tend to her mother alone in the dark house. She faces fear, considers going after him, but chooses to stay, pray, and care for her mother. Gives her a decision, action, and small arc.

### Edit 2: Make Guille's refusals agonizing
**Resolves**: characters[2], authenticity[3]
At door 2: Guille steps forward, hand reaching for the bread, stomach growling painfully — then his mother's words about prayer pull him back. At door 1: his hand almost reaches for the coin before he pulls it away.

### Edit 3: Differentiate the two tempters
**Resolves**: characters[3], authenticity[4]
The woman: seductive, patient, wheedling — "¿No quieres sentarte? Solo un poco..." The blacksmith: blunt, contemptuous, mocking — he doesn't offer gently, he challenges. Different rhetorical strategies testing different weaknesses.

### Edit 4: Make blacksmith's demand concrete for 5yo
**Resolves**: audience[3], audience[1]
Replace "las manos de dentro, la capacidad de hacer cosas" with concrete actions: "nunca más podrás abrazar a tu hermana, ni encender un fuego, ni partir el pan." Also trim forge description ~30%.

### Edit 5: Rework the hermit's speech — shorter, theologically precise
**Resolves**: values[1], authenticity[7], theologian[1], theologian[2], audience[5]
Shorten to: "Has rechazado lo que te habría costado el alma. Te pidieron que vendieras lo que no es tuyo sino de Dios. Eso fue sabio." For prayer: "rezar es hablar con Dios, que siempre escucha, aunque todo diga lo contrario." This fixes the selling-vs-sacrifice ambiguity and adds the petitionary dimension.

### Edit 6: Fix "máquina" anachronism
**Resolves**: prose[1]
Replace with medieval-appropriate: "No porque la oración sea una moneda que se echa en un platillo para que caiga un milagro" or integrate more naturally into the revised hermit speech.

### Edit 7: Add consequences for tempters + door-vanishing detail
**Resolves**: values[2], structure[4]
After Guille exits door 2: the woman's voice fades to a thin whine behind the wood. After door 1: the forge's glow dims behind the iron, and the caged birds fall silent. The hermit can briefly contextualize: they are always there, always offering the same bargain.

### Edit 8: Dramatize the candle-extinguishing scene
**Resolves**: structure[2], characters[4]
Replace the reported-speech version with a brief narrated scene: the night the mother dies, Guille wakes to darkness, the candle is out, the house is cold. He sits in the dark. Then he gets up and relights it.

### Edit 9: Rework the epilogue — shorter, one concrete scene
**Resolves**: audience[2], craft[2,3], authenticity[1,6], prose[3]
Instead of summarizing decades, show ONE brief scene of grown Guille — e.g., carrying flour home while Isa reads by the door. Then the candle-extinguishing scene. Then Isa asks about the candle. Cut the explicit aphorism entirely. End on the candle still burning.

### Edit 10: Cut explicit moral statements
**Resolves**: craft[1], values[3], authenticity[1,2]
- Cut "No fue un milagro con trompetas y ángeles..." — let the neighbor chain speak for itself
- Cut "que lo que más vale es lo que no se puede comprar..."
- Reduce refrain to 2 occurrences, end on "alguien la volvería a encender"

### Edit 11: Fix early candle simile
**Resolves**: craft[4]
Change "como una vela que se va apagando" → "como el último hilo de humo de un fuego muerto"

### Edit 12: Enhance woman's full description
**Resolves**: craft[5], prose[2]
Change "labios rojos como una herida" → "labios rojos como la sangre fresca" (authentic Grimm). Make dress/necklace carry menace: dress moves without wind, pearls cold like river-stones.

### Edit 13: Add friction to neighbor-helping
**Resolves**: authenticity[5]
One neighbor (e.g., the miller) grumbles or hesitates before eventually helping too.

### Edit 14: Motivate community response
**Resolves**: structure[3]
Guille, emboldened by hope, goes out at dawn to ask for help — or the candle's light is established as unusually bright/visible. Combine with edit 13.

### Edit 15: Expand return journey
**Resolves**: structure[5]
Add 2-3 sentences: the forest sounds are the same but they don't frighten him now. The trees seem to lean back.

### Edit 16: Sharpen food list
**Resolves**: prose[4]
"pasteles con azúcar por encima" → "tartas de manzana con azúcar quemada por encima"

### Edit 17: Fix bird-cage simile
**Resolves**: prose[5]
"como si cantaran para no olvidar que alguna vez habían volado" → "pero sus cantos eran tristes, como los de un pájaro que recuerda el cielo"

### Edit 18: Add act of faith before third door
**Resolves**: theologian feedback
Brief moment: Guille looks at the last door, crosses himself, whispers a prayer, and enters.

### Edit 19: Tighten door scenes for pacing
**Resolves**: audience[4]
Trim descriptive padding in both door scenes while keeping dialogue and decisions. Net reduction ~200 words from middle.
